How does it work?

Workshop for the manufacture of jewelry of any
complexity exactlyaccording to your idea.

  1. tell the idea to details to our master in our caffee
  2. admire the result :)

Breath the life in your intention

Stages of preparation:

  1. Individual consultation in a format convenient for you
    Via the Internet (zoom or voice-up) or meeting with the master in person in the cafeteria. From you: free time and an approximate sketch (don't worry, our masters are excellent artists, with your sketch and description we will achieve the perfect result together), from us: coffee and time of the master.
  2. Project sketch and prepayment
    Our master will try to send you an approximate sketch with a description of the materials as soon as possible, on average it takes 3-5 days (in case of urgency, additional payment will be required, the master should not miss a single detail, so he will devote all his time to your project).
  3. Materials and final cost
    Discussion of materials and the final cost of the project. The master will offer you the most suitable materials together with the sketch, or, if they were announced earlier, only confirmation of the master's sketch will be required. The master will also announce the final cost of the project.
  4. Making the latest edits
    Craft-time!
