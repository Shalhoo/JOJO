How to take care about yor jewelry

Adverse effecton of environment

Doing hard work/handiwork better take of your jewelry. Protect if from strainings or metal part may disorted and fossils damaged
Avoid the contact of it with cosmetic(cream, lotion, perfume, etc). The contact can effect on color of gem and decoration
Keep off abluents - the composition can leave spos on your treasure
Avoid intensive ultraviolet lights and high temperatures. Straight lights can spoil the initial view of your jewelry
Jewelry decorated by turquoise, amber, pearl, coral under the chemical and sunray can loose it brilliance and bloom

Handling

Dirty jewelry with gem need to be cleaned via special cleansers that you can buy in our workshop. Be acquainted with the inserted instruction before startbr
After swimming in the sea take care of your treasures via fresh water. Seasult and iodine in the sea can affect badly on your treasurebr
Jewelry need to be rubbed by suede, flannel or special jewelry rubs regularly. It helps it shine sleekybr
Silver items are recommended to clean with dry clot or soft brush, soaked with warm soapy waterbr
To make the diamonds glossy it needs to be washed in warm soapy water

Storage

Keep your items in cases in dry place. Necklace and chains better keep in separate cases to avoid creasesbr
Do not admit contact between different jewelry to avoid scratches
